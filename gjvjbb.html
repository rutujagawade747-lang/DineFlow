Restaurant Business Plan Sample

A simple structure for presenting your restaurant strategy, operations, and growth goals.

1. Overview

  • Restaurant concept
  • Target audience
  • Location and theme

2. Menu Plan

  • Signature dishes
  • Pricing strategy
  • Seasonal specials

3. Operations

  • Kitchen workflow
  • Staffing plan
  • Supplier list

4. Marketing

  • Social media presence
  • Local promotions
  • Customer loyalty

5. Financial Plan

  • Startup budget
  • Projected revenue
  • Break-even point

6. Timeline

  • Launch prep
  • Soft opening
  • Full operation
